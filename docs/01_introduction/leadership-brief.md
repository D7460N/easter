# Leadership Brief: Guiding Stage Ministry Decisions

## Purpose

To help pastors and ministry leaders make informed, prayerful decisions about pageant scope, volunteer load, and creative direction.

## Decision Priorities

1. **Mission Alignment** - Every performance must reinforce the church's vision and theology.
2. **Stewardship** - Consider budget, facility use, and volunteer capacity before committing to scope or schedule.
3. **Safety & Sustainability** - Favor smaller, consistent excellence over large, exhausting undertakings.
4. **Accessibility** - Aim for clarity of message; avoid complexity that distracts from the Gospel.

## Leadership Checklist

- [ ] Script/theme approved by pastoral team
- [ ] Budget & resources confirmed
- [ ] Volunteer coordinator assigned
- [ ] Safety plan reviewed
- [ ] Post-event evaluation scheduled
